Interesting Topics

From passive to active intelligence

Understanding the world involves not only observing from a third person perspective, first-hand interaction is very important to construct a world model.

From perception to reasoning to action planning

It's unclear how we can construct the higher order cognitive functions from the rudimentary low-level features.

Biologically plausible model and training

Backpropagation is amazing with well-defined objectives and small network. However, it's very hard to imagine how to scale it up to brain level in life long training setting, where lobal gradient seems impractical.
